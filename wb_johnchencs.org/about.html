About Me
You can edit text on your website by double clicking on the text box on your website, alternatively when you select the text box a settings menu will appear, selecting Edit Text from this menu will also allow you to edit the text on your website.
 
If you have any questions, please visit our Support Center: help.websitebuilder.com. We have lots of helpful articles that will assist you in creating the website of your dreams!
Sample Text:

"Studio Architects is a Kansas based practice and we pride ourselves on our diverse portfolio, including projects in the sport, leisure, retail, residential and commercial sectors.  

We believe that building strong client relationships is incredibly important and we work closely with our clients to engender complete trust. 

We see diversity as our strength, and this has ensured that we've been a practice of choice for more than 10 years. 

Our team has a great wealth of experience and know that, despite the complexity of the construction process, there should be no mystery to the client.  We aim to make our designs as clear and easy to understand as possible."

 
This whole area is fully editable and gives you the opportunity to explain who you are.
 
Areas you might want to discuss in this section include: 
 
  • What is your background?
  • Do you work alone or as a team?
  • What other credentials do you have?
  • How many years have you been in business?
  • What areas do you specialise in?
  • What are some of the projects you've worked on?
  • What made you want to want to be an Architect?
 
What you write is totally up to you, there is no right or wrong way to complete your 'About Us' page.  We advise making your language friendly and approachable, while being informative and professional.
